## FINAL 9
Cetus 6/20/2024 11:52 AM
FINAL 9 - Original OC Do Not Steal! In this competition, each player will be making their very own Hamster OC! This challenge comes in two parts with the first part being illustrating a unique hamster character using a single, randomly given category as a guideline, and then the second part being a backstory and character description. Your submissions will be scored by Cetus, who will not know who made which hamster. As a result you're not allowed to put anything in your hamster sona or backstory that will give away your identity. The hosts can transcribe the backstory if they feel the writing style is to distinct to a single player. Each hamster will be ranked from worst to best with player who has the best designed hamster receiving immunity! Now, I'm not looking for incredible art or writing skills I'm looking for something creative and unique that interprets the one guideline category in a fun way. For example, if you're given the category "Shakespeare", drawing up a hamster that's just William Shakespeare is fun but doesn't do much with the category. However, if you illustrate a hamster with two bisecting colors representing Romeo and Juliet who seems to be in some sort of split personality in conflict with herself then... That's AWESOME! So long as you both adhere to the guideline and present a unique character with an interesting, or funny, backstory and design then you'll be ranked highly. We'll have a pool of 9 guideline words. The word your receive will depend on who starts the challenge first and last. However, they'll be hidden from view and you'll only know your own word. Please don't discuss your idea with other players or I'll kill you. (edited)
