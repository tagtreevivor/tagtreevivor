F14- Rules Rule!

The aim of this Challenge is to get AS LEAST points as possible Each tribe will have to figure out 10 secret rules! These rules all vary in difficulty and how specific they are, however if there are any really specific rules, you can ask for hints so it's not borderline impossible to solve, however hints will cost you points. There will be two channels open for this challenge, one to submit your guesses and messages to be checked for in each rule, and the other to discuss what the rule could be. HOW GUESSING WORKS Every message your tribe sends in the submission channel will be checked if it does or does not follow the rule, the only time it doesn't is if you go for a guess of what the rule may be, by saying "GUESS: [answer]", you'll have a limit of 5 guesses per rule to do so this repeats until the 10th message, that isn't a guess, where your tribe uses up all your remaining guesses. All messages in the guessing channel from your tribe, including guesses, will be 1 point, if there are any messages that exceed the limit of messages or guesses, then it will add 2 points instead of just 1 HOW DISCUSSION WORKS In the discussion channel and ONLY the discussion channel, you get to talk to the rest of your tribemates that are doing the challenge about what messages to submit and what the rule could be, DO NOT SUBMIT ANY ANSWERS IN THIS CHANNEL Also in the discussion channel you can ask for hints, each rule has 2 hints, the first hint will tell you if the rule is to with words (e.g specific words), letters (e.g amount of letters), or numbers (e.g the amount of words has to be a square number), the second hint would be more specific to the rule and could vary from being a valid message following the rule, to something that relates to the rule itself! First Hint- 1 point Second Hint-3 points (edited)
